Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Quran learning programs

General Questions

What is Online Quran Pak Academy?

Online Quran Pak Academy is a professional online platform offering Quran education to students worldwide. We provide one-on-one classes with certified teachers, flexible scheduling, and comprehensive courses for all levels.

Who can join your Quran classes?

Our classes are suitable for everyone - children (age 5+), adults, beginners, and advanced learners. We have separate male and female teachers available for different age groups and learning levels.

Do you offer free trial classes?

Yes, we offer a free trial class for all new students. This allows you to experience our teaching methodology, meet your teacher, and assess our platform before making any commitment.

What are your operating hours?

We operate 24/7 to accommodate students from different time zones. You can schedule classes at any time that suits you, including weekends and holidays.

How do I book a class?

You can book a class by contacting us via WhatsApp, email, or through our website. We'll schedule a free trial class at your preferred time and assign a suitable teacher.

Courses & Learning

Which course should I start with as a beginner?

For complete beginners, we recommend starting with our Noorani Qaida course. It teaches Arabic alphabet, basic pronunciation, and foundational rules that are essential for Quran reading.

How long does it take to complete a course?

The duration varies depending on the course and student's learning pace. Typically: Noorani Qaida (2-4 months), Quran Nazra (6-12 months), Hifz program (2-3 years). We provide personalized learning plans.

Do you provide course materials?

Yes, we provide all necessary digital course materials including Noorani Qaida books, Tajweed rules, practice sheets, and audio recordings. These are included in your course fee.

Can I switch courses during my learning?

Absolutely! You can switch courses or upgrade your plan at any time. Our academic advisors will help you choose the right path based on your progress and goals.

Do you offer certificate after course completion?

Yes, we provide certificates upon successful completion of each course. For Hifz program, we also arrange Ijazah certification for qualified students.

Technical Requirements

What equipment do I need for online classes?

You need a computer/laptop/tablet with webcam, stable internet connection, and headphones. We recommend using Google Chrome browser for best experience.

Which platforms do you use for classes?

We use Zoom, Skype, and our custom learning platform. You can choose whichever platform you're comfortable with. We provide guidance for setup if needed.

What if I face technical issues during class?

We have technical support available 24/7. You can contact us via WhatsApp, email, or phone, and our team will immediately assist you with any technical problems.

Can I take classes on mobile phone?

Yes, you can take classes on smartphones through Zoom or Skype apps. However, we recommend using tablets or computers for better learning experience.

What internet speed is required?

A minimum of 2 Mbps download and 1 Mbps upload speed is recommended for smooth video classes. Better speed ensures higher quality video and audio.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and local payment methods depending on your country. All payments are secure and encrypted.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our services within the first week, we provide a full refund.

Can I pay monthly or do I have to pay for the whole course?

We offer flexible monthly payment options. You pay month-to-month and can pause or cancel anytime. No long-term commitment required.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. The price you see includes all classes, course materials, and support. We believe in transparent pricing.

Do you offer discounts for siblings or multiple courses?

Yes, we offer family discounts for multiple siblings and special packages for students taking multiple courses. Contact us for customized pricing.

What happens if I miss a class?

We understand emergencies happen. You can reschedule missed classes by informing us 2 hours in advance. We're flexible with scheduling make-up classes.
